FAQ

Find the answers to the most frequently asked questions about GatherWise.
We make it easy to get tickets for events, because We Gather for Good!

How do I buy tickets?

It's simple! Browse events on our homepage, select your preferred event, and purchase your tickets. You'll receive an email confirmation with your ticket details.

Is it safe to purchase tickets online?

Absolutely! GatherWise partners with trusted payment providers to ensure your transactions are secure.

Can I cancel my tickets?

Unfortunately, ticket cancellations are not supported. Please double-check your selection before purchasing.

What happens if an event is postponed?

If an event is postponed, you will be notified via email with the updated details. Your tickets remain valid.

Are there discounts for bulk ticket purchases?

Yes! Contact our support team to learn about discounts for group purchases.

Who can I contact for support?

Our support team is available 24/7 via the contact form on our website or by email at support@gatherwise.com.
